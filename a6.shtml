Assignment 6: Generative Form

build a generative aesthetic representation of a self-chosen dataset

Choose your own data set from:

  • Personal
  • Your research/thesis
  • Explain a complex concept
  • Explore an open-ended story

Design and build a generative aesthetic representation of your chosen data set. Your representation may or may not be legible but it should convey some aspect (e.g. emotional, complexity, dynamicism, rhythm, etc.) of the data set.
